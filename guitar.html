3D Printed Electric Guitar

Time: September 2023 - Present
Role: Sole creator
Tools: Hand tools, SolidWorks, 3D Printing

SUMMARY

This is a personal project built by myself, with the original model stemming from the Prusa Guitar. The goal is for the body of the guitar to be 3D printed using only a Prusa and PLA filament. It will be a functioning electrical guitar with a unique design.
